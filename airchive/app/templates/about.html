API
You can submit your query to the system and take responses. Query should have the following format:
http://airchive.logismi.co/getdata/?quantity=MeasuredQuantity&fromtime=FROMDATE&totime=TODATE&format=FORMAT&filter=FILTER&granularity=Filter's granularity
Where MeasuredQuantity is one of :

  • Temperature
  • Pressure
  • Nitrogen_Dioxide
  • Carbon_Monoxide
  • Volume

Format is one of:

  • csv
  • GeoRSS
  • GeoJSON

For more information regarding quantities, sensors, stations, filters and time boundaries click here

Fromtime and Totime has the following structure:
YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS
